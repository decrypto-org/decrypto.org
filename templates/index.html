Our group

Decrypto is a decentralized and independent research group focusing on blockchain research. We run semester-long research projects in affiliation with many universities. Many of our research papers have stemmed out of bachelor or master theses. Our work is peer-reviewed and appears in many top conferences of the field.

Seminar

We run a weekly seminar in which leading researchers in the field present a research-level topic in the field of blockchain science. Our seminars are held online and are open to all.

Bachelor and Master thesis proposals

If you are a student looking for a challenging and rewarding thesis topic, head over to the project proposals, pick your poison choose a topic that you like and contact the responsible person. Some projects are funded!
